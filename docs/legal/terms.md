Sign-in is by emailed magic link; there are no passwords. You are
responsible for access to your mailbox. You can delete your account at any

## Fair play

Ratings, percentiles and daily ranks assume a human solver. Submitting
machine-generated solutions to rated boards, tampering with solve timing, or
abusing the API may make your solves ineligible for rankings and may end
your account. The puzzles themselves are free to discuss and spoil once the
day's board has rolled over.
